About us:


At Innovation Squad Hotel, we are dedicated to redefining the classic hotel experience and delivering unparalleled hospitality. Nestled in the heart of the city, our hotel offers a perfect blend of modern sophistication and timeless elegance. Whether you are traveling for business or leisure, our team of committed professionals is here to ensure your stay is nothing short of exceptional

Accommodations

Step into our well-appointed rooms and suites, designed to provide ultimate comfort and relaxation. Each room boasts contemporary furnishings, plush bedding, and state-of-the-art amenities to cater to your every need. Whether you choose a standard room or a luxurious suite, you can expect a restful stay in a setting that exudes refinement.

Dining

Indulge your senses in our diverse culinary offerings. The Innovation Squad Hotel features a range of dining options, from casual cafes to elegant restaurants. Our talented chefs skillfully prepare a variety of cuisines using the finest ingredients, ensuring a delightful dining experience for every palate.

Facilities

Our hotel offers an array of facilities to enhance your stay. Take a refreshing dip in the rooftop swimming pool, enjoy a revitalizing workout at the fully-equipped fitness center, or unwind with a soothing massage at our spa. For the business traveler, we offer well-equipped meeting and conference rooms, complemented by our efficient business services.

Hospitality

At the heart of our hotel is a commitment to exceptional hospitality. From the moment you step through our doors until your departure, our attentive staff will cater to your every need, ensuring a seamless and unforgettable stay.


Experience the charm of classic hospitality with a touch of innovation at Innovation Squad Hotel. Whether you're here for business or leisure, we promise an experience that will exceed your expectations and leave you with cherished memories. Book your stay with us and embark on an extraordinary journey of luxury and comfort.
